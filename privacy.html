Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Caribbean Chronicles by JOS ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and subscribe to our newsletter and podcast.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address and name (if provided).
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To send you our weekly newsletter and podcast updates
  • To improve our website and content
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Beehiiv: Our newsletter is hosted and distributed through Beehiiv. Your email address is stored on their servers. Please review Beehiiv's Privacy Policy.
  • Podcast Hosting: Our podcast is distributed through various platforms including Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube, Pandora, and iHeartRadio. Each platform has its own privacy policy.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our website and newsletter (e.g., Beehiiv)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information.
  • Unsubscribe: You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
